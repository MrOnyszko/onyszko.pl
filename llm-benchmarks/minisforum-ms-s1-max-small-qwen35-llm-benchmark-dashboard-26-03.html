🔑 Key Finding: 4B vs 9B Performance

The qwen3.5-4b models significantly outperform their 9B counterparts in generation speed — nearly 2x faster at peak (49 vs 31 TPS for Q4). However, 9B models show slightly better TTFT at higher context fills. The 4B models are ideal for fast iteration while 9B-per-parameter offers better quality for reasoning tasks.

⚠ The TTFT Paradox at 90–95% Fill

Counterintuitively, TTFT drops at 90–95% fill vs 75% fill for most models. This is a KV cache paging / chunked prefill artifact: at very high fill ratios, the scheduler processes fewer new tokens per step, so individual step latency looks lower. Don't be fooled — absolute latency is still high at these context sizes.
